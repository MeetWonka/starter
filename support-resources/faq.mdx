# FAQ

This page will contain a list of frequently asked questions and their answers.


## Common First Questions

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How do I switch between AI models?" icon="toggle-on">
Use the model selector in the top bar of the interface. Choose "Thinking" for complex tasks requiring deep analysis, or "Non-Thinking" for quick, straightforward queries.

You can change models mid-conversation if needed.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can WonkaChat access all my company data?" icon="lock">
WonkaChat only accesses tools and data you explicitly connect. You control which systems are integrated and what permissions WonkaChat has within each system.

All access follows your organization's security policies and user permissions.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if I make a mistake or want to undo something?" icon="rotate-left">
WonkaChat shows you what actions it plans to take before executing them (especially for write operations). You can review and confirm before changes are made.

For audit and rollback capabilities, see your organization's governance settings.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I teach WonkaChat about my business?" icon="graduation-cap">
You can upload documents, share company knowledge, and provide context through conversation. The platform learns your terminology and processes over time.

For detailed guidance, see the [Business Teaching](/ai-agents/business-teaching) section.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Where can I get help if I'm stuck?" icon="life-ring">
- Check the [FAQ](/support-resources/faq) for common questions
- Visit [Troubleshooting](/support-resources/troubleshooting) for solutions
- Contact your organization's WonkaChat administrator
- Use the [Getting Help](/support-resources/getting-help) resources
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
