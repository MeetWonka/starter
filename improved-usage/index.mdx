# Introduction to Improving Your Usage

There are three main ways to improve your usage of AI and Wonka Chat:

1.  **Asking Great Questions**: The foundation of any good AI interaction.
2.  **Prompt Engineering**: Structuring your requests for the best possible output.
3.  **Conversation Management**: Guiding the AI through a coherent dialogue.
